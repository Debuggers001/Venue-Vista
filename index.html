About Us

Our Project, Venue Vista, addresses the prevalent issue of cumbersome venue selection for community events. By leveraging Google Maps, the platform streamlines the process, offering venue suggestions based on customizable filters, real-time availability, and user reviews. This tool empowers community members to plan successful events, fostering greater engagement and collaboration within local neighborhoods.
